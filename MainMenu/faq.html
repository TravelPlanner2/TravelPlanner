Frequently Ask Questions

  • What is TravelPlanner?

    TravelPlanner is a very useful tool that is used to plan trips in an easy and organized way. It also collects feedback from travelers to improve the plans of future members.

  • What language are available?

    At the moment TravelPlanner is only available in English, but in the future it will have many more.

  • I have technical problem, who do I email?

    If you have any technical problem you can contact us by filling out a contact form through the "Contact" section that you can see at the top of the page, you can also write an email directly to info@sde404.com for more information.

  • Can I cancel a trip that is already taken?

    Yes, you can cancel the plan/trip at any time with a minimum notice of 24 hours before the trip. Once this limit has been passed, unfortunately it will not be possible to cancel the plan/trip.

  • How do I use TravelPlanner planner?

    To use the TravelPlanner planner it is as simple as going to the "Planner" section that you can find at the top of the website. Once there, you can start planning your dream trip.

  • Is TravelPlanner free??

    Yeah. TravelPlanner is completely free and can be used unlimitedly. You only have to pay the cost of the trip once it is planned, nothing more.
